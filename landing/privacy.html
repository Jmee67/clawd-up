Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version

We collect almost nothing. The landing page has no analytics, no tracking pixels, no cookies. When you buy Clawd Up, LemonSqueezy handles the checkout and payment. We only use your email to deliver your license key.

What we collect

  • Email address — collected by LemonSqueezy during checkout, used solely to deliver your license key and purchase receipt.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by LemonSqueezy. We never see or store your card details.

What we don't collect

  • No analytics or tracking on this website
  • No cookies
  • No third-party scripts beyond Tailwind CSS (styling only)
  • No usage telemetry in the product

How we use your email

Your email is used for one thing: delivering your license key after purchase. We won't add you to a mailing list, send marketing emails, or share your email with anyone.

Third parties

LemonSqueezy processes payments and handles checkout. Their privacy policy governs data collected during the purchase process. We don't share your data with anyone else.

Data retention

We retain your email and purchase record for as long as your license is active, or as required by law. If you want your data deleted, email us and we'll handle it.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top. That's it.
