# Opportunity Scoring Rubric

Score each opportunity across 5 dimensions (1-5 scale). Total out of 25.

## Dimensions

### 1. Market Signal Strength (1-5)
- **5:** Multiple explicit "would pay for this" signals, growing trend
- **4:** Clear pain point with evidence of willingness to pay
- **3:** Moderate signal â€” people complain but unclear if they'd pay
- **2:** Weak signal â€” one-off mentions, no pattern
- **1:** No real signal â€” you're guessing

### 2. Competitive Gap (1-5)
- **5:** No existing solution, or existing ones are terrible and expensive
- **4:** Solutions exist but miss key segment or feature
- **3:** Moderate competition, room for differentiation
- **2:** Crowded space, hard to differentiate
- **1:** Dominated by well-funded incumbents

### 3. Build Feasibility (1-5)
- **5:** MVP in <1 week, solo buildable, tech you know
- **4:** MVP in 1-2 weeks, mostly known stack
- **3:** MVP in 2-4 weeks, some learning required
- **2:** 1-2 months minimum, significant unknowns
- **1:** Major technical challenges, team required

### 4. Revenue Potential (1-5)
- **5:** Clear path to $10K+ MRR, strong unit economics
- **4:** $5-10K MRR achievable, good margins
- **3:** $1-5K MRR possible, decent margins
- **2:** <$1K MRR likely, or thin margins
- **1:** Unclear monetization, free-expectation market

### 5. Founder-Market Fit (1-5)
- **5:** Deep domain expertise, existing audience, passionate
- **4:** Good knowledge, some audience, interested
- **3:** General familiarity, willing to learn
- **2:** Outside comfort zone, no existing advantage
- **1:** No relevant experience or interest
